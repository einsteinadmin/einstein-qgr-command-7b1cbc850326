How to read this dashboard

  • Success rate = (Super Green + Green) / (Super Green + Green + Red). Ungraded rocks are excluded from the denominator — they represent goals that weren't scored, not failures.
  • Heat map colors: Dark green = 80%+ (may be too safe), medium green = 70-80% (target zone, dashed outline), yellow = 50-70%, orange = 30-50%, red = below 30%, gray = no graded data.
  • Card borders: Green = healthy (65-80%), yellow = too safe (above 80%), red = problem (below 65%).
  • Trailing success % = all-time across every quarter shown, weighted by rock count.
  • Trend arrows compare the last two quarters with graded data.
  • Einstein quarters are offset: Q1 = Feb/Mar/Apr, Q2 = May/Jun/Jul, Q3 = Aug/Sep/Oct, Q4 = Nov/Dec/Jan.
