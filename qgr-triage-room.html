How this room works

    
Cameron + Paul's red-pen session · four tabs, in order · everything auto-saves as you click

    
    
      
  1. Q2 Review — for each rock, click a PROJ grade (SG / G / R). SELF grades are pre-seeded from the leaders' own AARs; an orange ⚠ DIVERGENCE border means your read disagrees with theirs — those are the ones to talk about. The notes box = notes for the room.
    2. 
      
  2. Q3 Triage — the main event. The room opens grouped by department: each department, then every person under it (the leader first, then their directs), then that person's goals as cards underneath them. Cards sort by effective owner, so something assigned to Nhel sits under Customer Experience › Nhel instead of buried in Amanda's lane. 👤 By leader lane switches to one lane per Roundtable leader, which is where the "+ Add a goal" row and the pool pills live. (There is also a ☐ Compact bars toggle that collapses every card to a one-line bar you click to expand — denser, but tiles are the default.) Now the per-card mechanics — walk each person top to bottom. Per card: pick a ruling — ACCEPT / EDIT / KILL / DEFER / ✓ DONE (EDIT opens a "what changes" box; ✓ DONE is for already-shipped work — the export sweeps it into a dedicated list for Albert to move to Completed on the Master Backlog) — toggle ★ CRIT (each leader needs exactly 2). Second row = placement: Leader / slate shows which lane the card lives in (only MB-pool cards can move lanes); Assign to (direct) is the specific person carrying it — secondary, optional, defaults to the leader. Also here: keep it in the Q3 shortlist or park it in overflow. Use the 👤 Filter by person / department panel to drill into just the people you want — check a whole department (e.g. Customer Experience) to see everyone's cards, or check individuals (Amanda alone, or Amanda + Nhel) to narrow further; leaders with nothing left to show drop out of the view, and a live count tracks the current selection. Separately, ☐ Group cards by owner clusters a leader's cards by who actually owns them (e.g. Amanda vs Tet vs Nhel vs Carly) instead of one flat list — the two work together. "+ Add a goal" at the lane bottom catches anything missing. Answer the 5 Open Questions chips up top as you go. Each card's Flywheel/PACe/priority lens tags are tucked under a "Lens detail" click-to-expand — the face stays clean, the data's one click away.
    3. 
      
  3. Consolidate — check the box on 2+ cards that are really the same thing, click ⛙ Consolidate in the header, name the merged card. The originals collapse into one; the export notes exactly which cards got folded in.
